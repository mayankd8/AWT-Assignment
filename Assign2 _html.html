RIP Irrfan Khan

On his struggle
I did experience frustration at some point.But I concentrated on getting comfortable with my craft instead. Though I didn’t enjoy doing television much, I used it as a learning ground. Envy and frustration make one feel caged. It’s been a personal battle not to fall into this trap because if you give in to it, the noose only gets tighter.

The flipside of being an actor
The flipside is that often one gets stuck in similar roles and an actor tends to get bored. You could stop enjoying your work. The other downside is that people get disconnected from reality. They start dwelling in a different space. Getting habituated to the recognition, doesn’t allow you to grow. The self-obsession is dangerous.
